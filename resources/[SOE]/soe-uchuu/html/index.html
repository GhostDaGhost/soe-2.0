SoE Tips and Tricks
  • • You can type /guide in-game to see a list of available commands and keybinds.

  • • Join our discord at soe.gg/discord for easily accessible announcements and updates.

  • • Each account is allowed 10 characters - Use them wisely since you're only allowed one account.

  • • If you'd like to delete an old, unused character (Or one which has died), you can do so at soe.gg/chardelete.

  • • The first thing you'll want is a phone! You can buy one around the corner from the Alta Hotel (Power St // Adams Apple Blvd).

  • • Need staff assistance? Use /a [message] to send a message to staff. Report should always be done on the forums.

  • • By visiting core.soe.gg, you will see an interactive web-panel useful for checking on your account and characters.

  • • Press F2 to access a radial menu with a number of options for your character, vehicle(s), and account.

  • • You can buy food and beverages at any 24/7 or convenience store.

  • • You can heal yourself at hospitals, but you will incur a much larger fee as opposed to if EMS/medical staff heal you.
