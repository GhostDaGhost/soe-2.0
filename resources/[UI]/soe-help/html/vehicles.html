Vehicles

[THIS SECTION IS A WORK IN PROGRESS] You want to get around in a timely manner, right? Who doesn't? You can't spend your whole life walking or biking around the state of San Andreas. You're going to want to buy a vehicle at some point, and they can be found at a number of dealerships around the state of San Andreas. Each dealership specializes in a different set of vehicles, so do your research. Below are some important icons to look for on your map.


Standard vehicle dealerships are where you can buy one of many vehicles available for purchase. Each dealership around the state specializes in a different type or class of vehicle, so make sure to do your research to determine which dealership you'll need to go to for a certain vehicle.


Motorcycle/bicycle dealerships! These do not HAVE cars.


Mod shops are repair shops are where you can fix and modify your vehicle(s) to your liking. Most shops offer a number of options for customization, including tint, paint, addon parts, and liveries.


Public garages are locations which you can store your vehicle for free. The valet will keep track of your vehicle and keep it safe while parked. They will not, however, drive it somewhere else, so you'll have to return to the same garage you parked your vehicle at in order to retrieve it.
