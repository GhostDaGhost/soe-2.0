Markets

In order to live in Los Santos, you may need some items to eat, drink, use, or defend yourself with. There are a number of different stores selling a number of different items across the state. Each store can be found on your map by the icon to the left of the description below.


These icons indicate a shop that you can buy food/drinks/groceries and many other things! Green means the store is a convenience store, thus that store sells food/drinks, makeup kits, first aid kits and fishing supplies! Pink means the store is a grocery store, thus that store would only sell groceries and food ingredients.


Is it already time to party? Come to the liquor stores for... liquor.


Hardware stores are your key to getting any kind of tools needed for any job. Not only do these stores sell tools such as crowbars, hammers, screwdrivers, and machetes, they also sell fishing rods and GPS devices for easy navigation. Prices are generally more expensive due to the more expensive products.


Clothing stores sell... clothes (surprise, surprise). You can stop by any clothing store for a quick change of clothing or to create a couple outfits to be used at a later point. Clothing changes are currently free.


Maybe you didn't like that buzzcut... or that mowhawk... maybe not even that Justin Bieber hair. Come to these shops to change/dye your hairstyle! Barbers are currently free.


If clothing isn't enough for you, you can get yourself a more permanent piece, a tattoo. Stop by any of the designated tattoo parlors in order to get a tattoo on any part of your body (Within reason of course). Tattoos are generally expensive, but can be cheaper if applied by a player tattoo artist.


Almost any vehicle will require gas at some point (Except those modern hippie cars). Stop by a gas station, open the F2 menu and hit Fuel Vehicle while facing your vehicle!


Need some painkillers? Forgot to grab a first aid kit on the way to "work"? Come to these pharmacies to grab what you need health-wise.


Damn... you left your keys in your car? Time to get a lockpick! Come to these locksmiths to grab lockpicks, handcuff keys and more!


You lost your gPhone into the toilet? Need a tablet? Come to these shops for your electronic device needs.


Time to defend your friends and family? Come to the gun stores for guns and ammo. Some will allow you to sell guns to them!


Same as a normal gun store but has a gun range to practice!


Fish markets will buy fish from you.


These pawn shops can vary. Some will buy electronics... some will buy any necklaces and watches. Who knows. Check the name in the map for the type.


Did you find out that you have no use for your deer pelts? What about that coyote meat? Come to the Paleto Lodge to sell them!


You can sell diamonds and such here. Blue diamond blip signifies Vangelico Jewelry Store.


If you don't yet have a vehicle (or yours is in the shop) and need to get around fairly quickly, do /bikerentals to find a rental spot. Bikes are free and can be returned at any rental kiosk.


The scrapyard is where vehicles you don't like go to die. :( In reality, you go there to scrap said vehicle for a lower price than when you first bought it. You will never see this vehicle again. Go with your vehicle to the BROWN circle.
