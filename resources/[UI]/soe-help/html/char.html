Starter Items

Upon character creation, you'll receive a couple snacks and drinks to help you survive from the hunger and thirst system. You were also given a gPhone and a state license. To "turn on" your phone, use it in your inventory. If you ever lose your gPhone, you can go to a Digital Den or any electronic store to get a new one. If you ever lose your state license, its really recommended to go get a new one at City Hall or the Paleto Welfare Office. You can be in certain trouble if you don't have an ID! Especially if it gets stolen by other players. To show your license to people nearby, use the ID in your inventory.
