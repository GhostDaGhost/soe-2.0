Why Get A Job?

In order to live long and prosper, you'll need some kind of income. The typical choice for most people is to get a job and get a paycheck. This can be done one of two ways. You can either apply for and work for a player-owned company or business, which you can find job advertisements for on the forums, or you can work for a local company doing any number of things. Below are some ways you can make money without being employed by another player.


Has anybody ever told you that you'd make a good hooker? The tow kind of course. The state is always hiring enthusiastic tow truck drivers to operate trucks for civilian and emergency services use. You can pick up a truck at any of the two depots marked on your map by the icon to the left. Once you have a truck, make sure you have a radio and tune into channel 5. You can also advertise for civilian tows.


There's always news to report on in San Andreas. From another police chase to a person claiming they've seen aliens, there's never a shortage of new material. Weazel News is hiring people to cover stories across the state. You can pick up a van at their Los Santos headquarters, marked on your map by the icon to the left. Once you have the van, it's up to you to find and report newsworthy content. This job is purely roleplay-based and gives you a chance to get alerts about activites!


Feel like you are fast enough to deliver some packages to properties? You can come get hired in GoPostal with these icons to the left. Use the F2 menu for any interacton with this job.


Mama Mia! Come to these icons on the left to deliver some pizza! Use the E key for any interaction within this job. Make sure you grab the pizza from the moped prior to delivering at the destination. You will be given a moped to handle your deliveries.


Into the garbage collecting business? Come to this icon on the left to get hired! Use the F2 menu for any interaction within this job. Try and dumpster dive too to find some junk or goodies!


Perhaps you want to practice being a cop before applications open? Merryweather is open for recruitment! Come to this icon on the left and get hired! Use the F2 menu for any interaction within this job. You'll be assigned to patrol several checkpoints on foot or in your security vehicle.


If you love hotdogs, I'm sure you'll like selling them out to people. Come to this icon on the left and use the F2 menu for any interaction within this job. You'll be given a hotdog cart to sell hotdogs!


Not an official job for hiring and such however you can go get a pickaxe from the hardware store and go mining at the Davis Quartz area and gain money when selling the ores at the docks and diamonds in San Andreas Ave. Press E when you have a pickaxe and facing a rock.


Just like mining. Hunting is not an official job. You can go and hunt down deers, coyote and other animals and grab their pelts and meat to sell or for dinner! Press F2 when facing a dead animal and when you have a knife to harvest them.
